NSFG 2022–2023 · 5,586 women surveyed · EA-4 — Depo-Provera

Most women who tried Depo-Provera
ended up walking away from it

Among women who ever used Depo-Provera and reported being unhappy with at least one birth control method, 71.4% named Depo specifically as a method they stopped because of dissatisfaction — the highest rate of any contraceptive in the survey.

How we get to the number

What about the other half? 514 Depo users are not in the last box because they were never unhappy enough with any birth control method to stop using it — or they stopped for other reasons (like wanting to get pregnant). They were simply never asked the follow-up question.
