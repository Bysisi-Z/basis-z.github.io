Emergency Contraception — Context & Patterns

Emergency contraception is used as a one-time backup, not a regular method — it does not appear in the EA-17 dissatisfaction question at all. This page shows what regular methods these 1,607 EC users stopped when they were unhappy.

How we get to the number
