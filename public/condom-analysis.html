Condoms — Who Walks Away

21.1% of condom users who reported method dissatisfaction named condoms as a reason to stop — suggesting most women who stop condoms do so for other reasons (switching to hormonal methods, relationship changes, etc.).

How we get to the number

2,658 condom users had no EA-17 data — they were either satisfied or stopped for other reasons.
