Vaginal Ring — Who Walks Away

54.9% of vaginal ring users who expressed dissatisfaction with any method cited the ring — the highest rate among all hormonal methods except Depo-Provera.

How we get to the number

201 ring users had no EA-17 data — they were either satisfied or stopped for other reasons.
