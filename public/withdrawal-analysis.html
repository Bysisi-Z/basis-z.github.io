Withdrawal — Who Walks Away

13.0% of withdrawal users who expressed dissatisfaction with any method cited withdrawal — a relatively low rate. Most women who stop using withdrawal do so for reasons other than dissatisfaction.

How we get to the number

1,869 withdrawal users had no EA-17 data — they were either satisfied or stopped for other reasons.
