Partner's Vasectomy — Permanent by Design

A partner's vasectomy is a permanent surgical procedure. Only 1 respondent in the entire survey listed it in EA-17, confirming this method does not generate dissatisfaction-based discontinuation.

How we get to the number

A vasectomy cannot be stopped by the woman herself — it is a permanent procedure. The near-zero rate here reflects the nature of the method, not satisfaction levels.
