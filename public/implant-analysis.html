Hormonal Implant — Who Walks Away

57.6% of implant users who expressed dissatisfaction with any method cited the implant — a majority. Despite being a long-acting reversible method, more than half of dissatisfied users named it.

How we get to the number

232 implant users had no EA-17 data — they were either satisfied or stopped for other reasons.
