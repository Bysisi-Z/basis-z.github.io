Birth Control Pills — Who Walks Away

28.9% of women who ever used the pill explicitly stopped using it due to dissatisfaction — 1,008 out of 3,485 users.

How we get to the number

The remaining 2,477 women either continued using the pill, stopped for other reasons (wanting to get pregnant, relationship change, etc.), or were never dissatisfied with any method.
